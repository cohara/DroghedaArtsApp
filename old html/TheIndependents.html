Exhibition: The Independents

A photographic exhibition by Shane Cowley, draws on memories and dramas of Narrow West Street, revealing the vibrant humanity of a once bustling shopping district of Drogheda . These photographs provided the inspiration behind The Street, the Upstate Theatre Project's dramatic enactment of the stories behind them at 6:30 & 8:30, each night Thurs 1 - Mon 5 May, on Narrow West Street.


Wednesday 30 April - Monday 5 May

Venue: Barlow House, Narrow West Street

Times: 10:00am - 5:00pm Wed 30 April - Fri 2 May

2:00pm - 6:00pm Sat 3 - Mon 5 May

Tickets: Free
