Inspired by the unique stories and heritage of Drogheda, the Coderdojo young programmers will demonstrate the games they have made on the Games Trail and in the ‘Chill Out room’ in Boomerang Café.


Droichead Dig

1. Droichead Dig Part 1 – a game made using Scratch where ancient poet Amergin loses his wallet in the mound of Millmount and the player has to find it. Made by Meabh Hickey & Shahin Houshidari. This game is based at Millmount, look out for the QR Code.

2. Droichead Dig Part 2- Oliver Cromwell mislays his Mace and Sword in the walls of St Laurence’s Gate. Under the pressure of time, the player must find it or loose their life. Made by Meabh Hickey & Shahin Houshidari. This game is based near Laurence Gate so look out for the QR Code.

3. Droichead Dig Part 3 St. Oliver Plunkett searches for the pieces of the true cross in St. Peter’s Church but time is running out. Made by Meabh Hickey & Shahin Houshidari. This game is based near St Peter’s Church so look out for the QR Code.

Trailer & Interview with the developers of Droichead Dig http://youtu.be/6ltwJdVaMGk


More Great Games!

4. Cromwellian Chaos – a game where Oliver Cromwell chases and tries to catch the citizens of Drogheda - made by Shauna Kelly, Carl Fogarty, Adam Clarke, Luke Ferris. Based at the Tholsel, look out for the QR Code.

5. The Lady that Died Twice - This is a game in development by one of the youngest CoderDojo members. Young Alex Killeen will tell you about the games ambitions in this interview. Based at St Peter’s Protestant graveyard, look out for the QR Code near the Barbican Centre.

6. Super Sleuth- answer the quiz questions and make your way through Drogheda. Find yourself at the Hub at Boomerang Cafe. Inspired by Inspired by Holly Meade, Megan Finnegan, Casey Donnelly Quinn & Leah Donnelly Quinn. Look out for the QR Codes.
